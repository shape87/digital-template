Impending Lava Death

  • High Concept: Get your character to jump from one platform to the other without falling in the lava.
  • Player Experience Goals: To immerse the player in a themed environment and display comcial animation.
  • Genre: Action Arcade
  • Game play: Jump from platform to platform to score points and keep your chracter alive. Press the arrow keys to move, up to small jump, and space to long jump. It is suggested to jump when the platforms move fast because they may throw you off.
  • Prototype goal: Loss Avoidance, High Score
  • Features: Immersive environment, addictive gameplay, quick paced fun.
  • Setting/Premise: A Preston decides to have a rousing picnic on top of a volcano but accidentally falls in when he his robot allergies go hay wire, lets all hope he doesn't get melted.
  • Story: A robot named Preston is enjoying a picnic when his robot allergies go of, luckily lands on moving platform, but needs to move in order to evade an impending gruesome death.
  • Target Audience: Casual gamers trying to have fun and appreciate simple games.
  • Play time: About 30 seconds.
  • Strategies: Be patient, getting points isnt everything, if the platform ismoving fast and off screen avoid jumping to the other side. Also, make sure to jump when the platform you are standing on is moving very fast.
