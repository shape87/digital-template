Unfortunate Picnic

  • High Concept: While going on a hike you suddenly realize that there are tons of wildlife trying to ave you for lunch.
  • Player Experience Goals: To feel natural in the environment and avoid the obstacle excercising depth and space perception.
  • Genre: Action Arcade
  • Game play: Avoid fast moving objects as they spawn more and more over time.
  • Prototype goal: Loss Avoidance
  • Features: Cool environments, addictive gameplay, quick paced fun.
  • Setting/Premise:
  • Story: A robot named Preston is enjoying a walk in the meadow, and partaking in his favorite hobby, counting blades of grass. His fun filled day out becomes a nightmare as eagles, polar bears, and lions start mass breeding and eating everything in sight. Lets hope he doesn't meet impending doom.
  • Target Audience: Casual gamers trying to have fun and appreciate simple games.
  • Play time: About a minute.
  • Strategies: Try to notice where the enemeis are spawning to have a greater likelihood of survival.
