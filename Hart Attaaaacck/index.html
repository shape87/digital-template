  • High Concept: You are having an unsettling nightmare when suddenly hearts start attacking you.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should not only understand how to navigate the environment but also how best to dodge the attacking hearts.
  • Genre: Action Arcade
  • Game play: Avoid fast moving objects as they spawn more and more over time.
  • Prototype goal: Loss Avoidance
  • Features: Cool environments, addictive gameplay, quick paced fun.
  • Setting/Premise: An adrogenous student is having a weird dream that they can't get out of. Unfrotunatley hearts start attacking them and they begin to question existence as they run on top of miscelaneous dream material.
  • Story: "I took too much NyQuil!!!" said the sexually ambiguous protagonist. As he/she run across a film noir esque environment, his/her eyes deceive him/her as he/she sees hearts come his/her way.
  • Target Audience: CAsual gamers trying to have fun and appreciate simple games.
  • Play time: About 10 seconds to a minute, if you're that good.
  • Strategies: Get to the low poriton of the terrain as you have a better chance of dodging random hearts.
